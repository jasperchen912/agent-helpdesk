# Structure Variants

Use these only when the default template would make the series feel repetitive or the premise clearly points elsewhere.

## Missed Connection

- Best for: delayed messages, wrong timing, almost-meet stories, softened regret.
- Beat pattern: opening interruption -> attempted catch-up -> second miss -> residue.
- Ending mode: leave one action unfinished rather than forcing reunion.

## Closed-Space Pressure

- Best for: elevators, taxis, meeting rooms, late-night kitchens, waiting rooms, stairwells.
- Beat pattern: confined setup -> social pressure rises -> one small disclosure -> exit with changed air.
- Use when the energy should come from proximity rather than plot sprawl.

## Object-Led Secret

- Best for: keys, receipts, packages, umbrellas, phones, uniforms, meal cards, photos.
- Beat pattern: ordinary object -> irregular detail -> investigation or avoidance -> revelation.
- Keep the object active across the story instead of mentioning it once.

## Family Obligation

- Best for: dinners, calls from home, care labor, sibling friction, inherited errands.
- Beat pattern: practical request -> emotional undercurrent -> choice under pressure -> altered relationship texture.
- Do not let the story turn into generalized family commentary.

## Light Uncanny

- Best for: one impossible call, repeated delivery, slightly wrong room, object returning, predictive glitch.
- Keep the world mostly ordinary.
- Explain less than a mystery story would, but give more setup than a poem would.
