# Golden Sample

Use this file as the style anchor for the flagship daily report. Do not copy phrases mechanically; match the pacing, density, and editorial restraint.

For a concrete full article example, also read `references/morning-column-final-sample.md`.

## What Good Looks Like

- The title is direct and high-density.
- The lead names the day's common thread instead of listing headlines.
- The top 3 stories each get one compact factual paragraph plus one short line on why the story matters.
- The article moves from the biggest shared macro signal to narrower sector stories.
- The close ends with one sentence of editorial synthesis, not a long conclusion.

## House Style

- Write in clean Simplified Chinese.
- Keep sentences moderately short.
- Prefer one idea per paragraph.
- Avoid filler transitions and dramatic adjectives.
- Sound like an informed editor, not a commentator trying to win an argument.

## Sample Shape

### Title

`《3月9日最该看的几件大事》`

### Lead pattern

Start with one sentence that names the common thread of the day.

Example pattern:

`今天真正值得看的，不是某一条单独刷屏的新闻，而是几条成本、政策和技术线索正在重新交叉。`

Follow with one or two sentences that translate that pattern into reader value.

### Body pattern

For each major story:

- first paragraph: what happened
- second sentence or short paragraph: why it matters

Keep these blocks compact. Do not let any single item dominate unless the user asks for a deep dive.

### Closing pattern

End with one sentence that compresses the day into a judgment.

Example pattern:

`如果把今天的新闻压缩成一句话，那就是全球竞争越来越像一场围绕成本、秩序和基础设施的长期拉锯。`
