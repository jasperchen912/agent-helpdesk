# Golden Sample

Use this file as the style anchor for the flagship daily report. Do not copy phrases mechanically; match the pacing, density, and editorial restraint.

For a concrete full article example, also read `references/morning-column-final-sample.md`.

## What Good Looks Like

- The title is direct and high-density.
- The lead names the day's common thread instead of listing headlines.
- The top 3 stories each work as semi-explainers with facts, key detail, and transmission.
- The article moves from the biggest shared public signal to narrower sector stories.
- The `经济投资风向标` is short, specific, and directional.
- The close ends with one sentence of editorial synthesis, not a long conclusion.

## House Style

- Write in clean Simplified Chinese.
- Keep sentences moderately short.
- Prefer one idea per paragraph.
- Avoid filler transitions and dramatic adjectives.
- Sound like an informed editor, not a commentator trying to win an argument.

## Sample Shape

### Title

`《3月9日最该看的几件大事》`

### Lead pattern

Start with one sentence that names the common thread of the day.

Example pattern:

`今天真正值得看的，不是某一条孤立刷屏的消息，而是政策、市场和国际风险三条线索正在同时抬高一天的判断门槛。`

Follow with one or two sentences that translate that pattern into reader value.

### Body pattern

For each major story:

- first block: what happened
- second block: concrete facts such as who did what, on what date, at what scale, or with what number
- third block: the specific point of tension, unresolved issue, or next step
- fourth block: why it matters now and where the effect transmits next

Keep these blocks compact but complete. The reader should understand the story without immediately needing another article. If a block does not contain at least one hard fact, it is probably still too abstract.

### 风向标 pattern

Add `2-4` compact signals after the main sections.

For each signal:

- name the signal clearly
- say what changed
- explain why it matters to the direction of policy, markets, or risk appetite
- stop before it turns into advice

### Closing pattern

End with one sentence that compresses the day into a judgment.

Example pattern:

`如果把今天的新闻压缩成一句话，那就是决定情绪的，不再是单条 headline，而是谁在重新定义成本、预期和安全边界。`
