# Fast Brief Template

Use this template for a shorter briefing-style output. This is for users who want a fast scan rather than a flagship article.

## Best Use Cases

- Morning brief
- Evening recap
- "Give me the quick version"
- Light-news days

## Structure

1. One title or up to three title candidates
2. One-sentence lead
3. `5-7` key items in descending importance
4. Optional short watchlist
5. Source summary

## Article Skeleton

### Title

- Prefer a direct format such as `《3月9日最该看的几件大事》`

### Lead

- One sentence only
- Name the day's dominant signal in plain language

### Key items

For each item include:

- `Headline`
- `What happened`: one compact paragraph or two short sentences
- `Why it matters`: one short sentence
- `Source / date`

Keep the whole piece tight. Most items should stay within `3-4` sentences total.

### Optional watchlist

- Add `2-3` developing items only when they may matter tomorrow

### Source summary

- List the main outlets or official sources used

## Guardrails

- Do not create sections unless they make scanning easier.
- Do not use long transitions or long conclusions.
- Prefer clarity and compression over flourish.
- If a user asks for a fixed title pattern, keep the title exact.
