# Evening Recap Template

Use this template for a late-day summary. This is for users who want to know what changed today and what deserves attention tomorrow.

## Best Use Cases

- Evening newsletter
- End-of-day WeChat post
- "What happened today"
- Market-close or post-work reading

## Structure

1. One title or up to three title candidates
2. One short lead on the day's dominant shift
3. `4-6` key developments from today
4. `What changed by nightfall`
5. `What to watch tomorrow`
6. Source summary

## Article Skeleton

### Title

- Prefer a direct evening format
- Example pattern: `《3月9日晚，最该看的几件大事》`

### Lead

- One short paragraph
- Explain what became clearer by the end of the day

### Key developments

For each item include:

- `Headline`
- `What happened today`
- `Why tonight it matters`
- `Source / date`

### What changed by nightfall

- Summarize the day's updated picture in `1-2` short paragraphs
- Emphasize what is different from the morning view

### What to watch tomorrow

- Add `3-5` concrete watch points
- Prefer specific meetings, earnings, launches, deadlines, votes, or risk points

### Source summary

- List the main sources used

## Guardrails

- Keep the article current to the end of the day.
- Do not rehash morning headlines unless the situation materially changed.
- Make the tomorrow watchlist concrete, not generic.
