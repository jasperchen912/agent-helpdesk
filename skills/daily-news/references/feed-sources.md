# Feed Sources

Use this file to keep the standing source mix for the morning column aligned with its editorial goal.

## Source Mix

Split the standing source set into three layers:

### 1. Macro and public-interest news

Use these to capture the shared agenda of the day:

- BBC Top Stories
- BBC World
- BBC Business
- BBC Technology

These feeds are broad enough to surface major world, policy, and market-moving developments before the article narrows into technology and business.

### 2. Chinese technology and business media

Use these to give the morning column a stable Chinese-language angle and improve topic selection for WeChat readers:

- 36氪 综合资讯
- 爱范儿 全文 RSS
- 少数派 Feed

These sources are useful for Chinese editorial framing, startup and platform coverage, product interpretation, and daily technology discussion that is closer to the reading habits of WeChat audiences.

### 3. Official company and platform updates

Use these to catch primary-source announcements in AI, platform, and major tech-company coverage:

- OpenAI News
- Google Research Blog
- Google DeepMind Blog
- Apple Newsroom
- Apple Developer News
- NVIDIA Developer Blog
- Hugging Face Blog

These feeds are valuable because they contain original product, research, and infrastructure announcements that are often worth citing directly or using as the primary confirmation layer.

## Source Weighting

Use the source set by editorial role, not as a flat pool.

### Tier A: lead-story candidates

These sources can supply stories that lead the article when the development has clear public, market, policy, or platform impact:

- BBC Top Stories
- BBC World
- BBC Business
- Official announcements or newsroom posts when the event itself is high-impact

Default use:

- choose the day's shared agenda
- support the lead paragraph
- supply 1-2 of the top 3 stories

### Tier B: section-story candidates

These sources are usually better for the `科技与商业` and `世界与政策` body sections than for the lead story:

- BBC Technology
- 36氪 综合资讯
- 爱范儿 全文 RSS
- 少数派 Feed
- Google Research Blog
- Google DeepMind Blog
- Apple Newsroom
- Apple Developer News
- NVIDIA Developer Blog
- Hugging Face Blog

Default use:

- enrich Chinese-language framing
- find section-worthy product, company, and ecosystem developments
- add texture after the macro agenda is set

### Tier C: confirmation or detail sources

These sources should often confirm or deepen an already selected story rather than define the whole column by themselves:

- official company blogs for niche product updates
- developer-facing feeds when the update is meaningful but not market-moving

Default use:

- confirm dates, product names, release scope, and quotes
- sharpen `为什么重要`
- supply detail for topic-focus variants

## Lead Selection Rules

- Do not let a company blog lead the whole morning column unless the event clearly changes markets, policy, platform power, or mainstream technology use.
- Prefer a macro/public-interest lead when several stories compete.
- Use Chinese tech media to improve framing and topic fit, but not as the sole basis for a major headline if stronger primary or international reporting exists.
- If the day is light, it is acceptable for a high-impact company announcement to enter the top 3, but it should still be written in a broad reader-facing frame.

## Editorial Use

- Do not treat every official blog post as a lead story.
- Do not let Chinese tech media overwhelm the macro agenda; use them to improve framing, topic fit, and Chinese-language readability.
- Use official feeds for confirmation, detail, and original framing.
- Keep the morning column balanced: macro agenda first, company and platform detail second.
- If an official announcement matters only inside one product ecosystem, it should usually stay in a section rather than become the headline of the day.

## Maintenance

- Prefer feeds that are stable, machine-readable, and close to the original publisher.
- If a feed becomes noisy or unreliable, replace it with another source in the same editorial role instead of expanding the feed list without bound.
- If a source changes quality, adjust its tier before removing it entirely.
