# Default Template

Use this structure for the standard nightly article. Keep the final output as one finished Markdown document. Treat it as the default scaffold, not a cage.

## Frontmatter

```markdown
---
title: 《浴室沉思｜YYYY.MM.DD｜题眼》
cover: ./assets/default-cover.jpg
---
```

## Body Rhythm

### Part 1: opening scene

- `80-160` Chinese characters.
- Open on one precise late-evening scene.
- Put a place, object, and action on the page within the first two sentences.
- Prefer concrete sensory details: mirror fog, tile seams, hallway light, queue silence, bus window reflection, sink water sound.

### Part 2: concrete observation

- `200-320` Chinese characters.
- Stay with what happened, what was seen, or what changed in the air.
- Build tension through detail instead of through argument.

### Part 3: inward turn

- `220-320` Chinese characters.
- Shift from outer scene to a shared inner recognition.
- Keep the reflection small and earned.

### Part 4: quiet landing

- `80-150` Chinese characters.
- End on a soft aftertaste, not a lesson.
- Favor one image, one pause, or one understated sentence.

## Assembly Rules

- Aim for `4` paragraphs by default. Allow `3-5` only when rhythm improves.
- Keep one dominant scene family. Add another scene only if it sharpens the same emotional residue.
- Let at least one paragraph imply another person, a public atmosphere, or a shared social pressure unless the piece is intentionally solitary.
- Save the sharpest sentence for the body, not the ending.
- Do not add section headings to the final article.
- Do not output alternate titles unless the user explicitly asks.
- Do not append explanation, prompt notes, or publishing instructions.
- If the user gives a fixed title or cover path, replace the defaults and keep the rest of the structure.
