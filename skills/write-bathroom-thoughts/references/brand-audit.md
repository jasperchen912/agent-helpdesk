# Brand Audit

Use this file to keep `《浴室沉思》` from drifting back into a generic公众号观点文。

## Current Positioning

The series should feel like a nightly observational essay with residual warmth, not like a listicle, emotional commentary post, or personal-growth explainer.

## What To Protect

- one physical scene before any argument
- one quiet recognition instead of several conclusions
- low-volume emotional truth
- specificity that can only come from lived observation

## What To Avoid Repeating

The launch-era pieces established publishing momentum, but they are not the long-term style target. Do not reuse them as tone models.

### Vol.01

`浴室沉思 Vol.01 | 朋友圈点赞，其实是成年人最低成本的“已阅”`

Why it is not the target model:

- built around a thesis rather than a scene
- depends on numbered sections and explicit argument steps
- resolves into direct social advice
- carries too much运营感 through互动和气泡模块

### Vol.02

`浴室沉思 Vol.02 | 为什么休假比上班还累？成年人的“报复性熬夜”与“无效休息”`

Why it is not the target model:

- topic choice is too close to a generic心理/效率内容号
- relies on platform-ready concepts instead of fresh observation
- over-explains the reader's state
- ends in uplift rather than residue

## Future Direction

Push the series toward:

- more scene, less thesis
- more residue, less takeaway
- more object and motion, less concept language
- more local endings, less advice

## Quick Test

If the draft could be retitled as "3个真相" or "为什么你总是..." without changing much, it is not yet `《浴室沉思》`.
