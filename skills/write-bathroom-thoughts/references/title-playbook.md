# Title Playbook

Use this file when choosing or revising the title.

## Archive Format

Use the default archive pattern unless the user supplies a fixed title:

`《浴室沉思｜YYYY.MM.DD｜{focus}》`

## Focus Selection Rules

- Keep the focus concrete.
- Prefer `6-14` Chinese characters.
- Favor one center of gravity: object, action, pause, sound, or tiny social movement.
- Let the title sharpen the scene, not summarize the message.

## Strong Focus Shapes

- one interrupted action
- one object at the end of the day
- one brief sound or light change
- one social hesitation between strangers

## Weak Focus Shapes

- abstract nouns such as `成长`, `生活`, `人生`, `答案`
- commentary framing such as `关于...`
- explicit moral framing such as `我们都需要...`
- compound titles that explain the whole essay before it starts

## Good Focus Examples

- 门快关上的那一下
- 还没放稳的湿伞
- 最后一格电梯灯
- 没发出去的那一句
- 钥匙落在桌上的声音

## Title Options Rule

- Return at most `3` options unless the user asks for more.
- Put the recommended option first.
- Keep all options inside the series voice. Do not brainstorm in unrelated styles.
